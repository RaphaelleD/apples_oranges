Summary

Improving Access to Psychological Therapies (IAPT) is run by the NHS in England and offers NICE-approved therapies for treating people with depression or anxiety.

This publication series makes available the most recent IAPT monthly data, including activity, waiting times, and outcomes such as recovery. It also includes experimental statistics from the Employment Advisor in IAPT pilot.
